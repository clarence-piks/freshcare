About Us

At Freshcare, we believe in bringing the best of nature to your table. Located in the heart of kampala, Namuwongo, we are passionate about providing fresh, high-quality fruits and vegetable to our community. Our produce is sourced directly from local farmers, ensuring that you get the freshest and most nutritious options. with our slogan "Fresh Everyday", we are commited to promoting healthy living and supporting sustainable farming practices. Join us in our mission to make fresh produce accessible to everyone!
